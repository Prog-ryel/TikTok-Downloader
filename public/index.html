How to Use

Steps to Download TikTok Videos:

  1. Get Video URL:
    • Open TikTok app or website
    • Find the video you want to download
    • Click "Share" and then "Copy Link"
  2. Paste URL:
    • Paste the copied link in the input box above
    • Or click the "Paste" button to automatically paste from clipboard
  3. Preview Video:
    • Click the "Preview" button
    • Wait for video information to load
  4. Choose Format:
    • Select MP4 for video download
    • Select MP3 for audio only
  5. Download:
    • Click the "Download" button
    • Wait for download to complete
    • File will be saved to your downloads folder

Note: Make sure to only download videos that you have permission to use.
